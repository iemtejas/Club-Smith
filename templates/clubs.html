Students' Welfare Department

In Club Smith, the Students' Welfare Department serves as a central hub for students, fostering their engagement in various facets of academic life. Its core mission revolves around cultivating a holistic university experience, promoting the harmonious development of body, mind, and spirit. This is achieved through a focus on extracurricular activities and out-of-classroom learning initiatives. Within this framework, the Students' Welfare Department oversees 18 clubs, each guided by faculty moderators from East West University. These clubs serve as platforms for students to explore and nurture their latent talents. Participation in club activities not only allows students to refine their interpersonal abilities through collaborative efforts but also fosters the growth of organizational and leadership skills. By providing these avenues for personal and professional development, Club Smith fulfills its role in enriching the university experience and elevating the institution's reputation.
